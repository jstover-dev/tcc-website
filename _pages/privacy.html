Privacy Policy

We collect the email addresses and other private information volunteered by those who communicate with us via email or through this website. This information is used for internal review only.

When submitting an enquiry on this website, you may be asked to provide private information such as your email address, name, address and phone number.

We will only ever use this information to reply to enquiries, it will never be shared with any third party.
Persons who supply us with contact details will only receive communications which directly relate to their enquiry.

We do not partner with or have special relationships with any online advertising companies.

We have appropriate security measures in place in our network and physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site.

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us through our contact page here.
